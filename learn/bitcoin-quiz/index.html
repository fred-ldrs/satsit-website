FAQ
What topics does the Bitcoin Trivia Quiz cover?
Questions span the full spectrum of Bitcoin knowledge: the history of Bitcoin and Satoshi Nakamoto, mining and halving events, wallets and private keys, the Lightning Network, notable price milestones, and Bitcoin fundamentals.
How many questions are included?
The quiz features a large pool of questions across multiple difficulty levels — Bitcoiner, Cypherpunk, and Satoshi — so every session is a fresh challenge whether you are new to Bitcoin or a long-time HODLer.
Is the Bitcoin Trivia Quiz free?
Yes, completely free. Play in the browser at bitcoin.triviaquiz.games or download the Android app from Google Play — no purchase or subscription required.
Can I play offline?
Yes. Install the PWA from your browser and all questions are cached locally for offline play. The Android app also works without an internet connection.
Can I submit my own Bitcoin trivia question?
Yes! Question submissions and community questions are currently handled via our quiz platform. A native submission feature is coming to Sat's It — stay tuned.
